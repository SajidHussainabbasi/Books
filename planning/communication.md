## Asking for Help

If you're stuck, follow this guide:

1. _0 → 30 min_: Investigate and debug on your own.
2. _30 → 60 min_: Ask teammates or peers for input.
3. _60+ min_: Reach out to mentors or coaches via Slack, Zoom, or GitHub.
